White Water Rafting

Dare to participate in this incredible rafting activity on the Urubamba River. You will be in contact with the beauty of nature and the landscapes of the Cusco mountains, with a lot of adventure and fun. During our full-day rafting tour on the Urubamba River we will run class II and III rapids and have a lot of fun with our crew and other participants for approximately 2.5 hours.
